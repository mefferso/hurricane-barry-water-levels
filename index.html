Data definitions & sources

Water level is the NOAA verified hourly height. Four coastal gauges are feet above MHHW.

Departure is observed minus the NOAA astronomical tide prediction.

Bayou Gauche exception: NOAA does not publish MHHW or tide predictions for station 8762482. It is shown in feet MSL; its anomaly is relative to the July 10 pre-storm mean.

Barry's hourly center, wind, and pressure are linearly interpolated between official NHC best-track fixes. The source fixes remain visible as small track dots.
